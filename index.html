Redmi Note 10 Pro

Redmi Note 10 Pro mobile was launched on 4th March 2021. The phone comes with a 6.67-inch touchscreen display. Redmi Note 10 Pro is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G processor. It comes with 6GB of RAM. The Redmi Note 10 Pro runs Android 11 and is powered by a 5050mAh battery. The Redmi Note 10 Pro supports proprietary fast charging.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Redmi Note 10 Pro on the rear packs a quad camera setup featuring a 64-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.9 aperture and a pixel size of 0.7-micron; an 8-megapixel camera; a 5-megapixel camera, and a 2-megapixel camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring a 16-megapixel sensor with an f/2.45 aperture and a pixel size of 1.0-micron.
